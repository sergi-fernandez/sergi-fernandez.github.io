Featured product cases

Product work where behavior, data, and operations have to line up.

01 / Driver performance products

Coaching products for measurable fleet outcomes

Driver performance and coaching products sit close to real operational behavior: fuel efficiency, safety, vehicle wear, driver learning, and fleet follow-up.

PM challenge
Translate vehicle and driver data into feedback that professional drivers and fleet managers can act on.
Actions
Shaped evaluation, coaching, lessons, quests, accomplishments, and fleet follow-up around connected driving data.
Outcome
Driver behavior can influence fuel consumption by up to 15%; the coaching model targets measurable fuel reduction.

02 / Shared mobility platform

Connected fleet services people can understand

An integrated mobility platform that turns connected vehicles and operational complexity into scalable digital services across booking, access, availability, subscriptions, and operations.

PM challenge
Make connected vehicles, operations, service rules, and user flows understandable in one platform experience.
Actions
Connected smart fleet management, digital key access, booking flows, subscription logic, and service operations.
Outcome
A scalable shared-mobility product system for carsharing, on-demand transport, and vehicle subscriptions.

03 / AI-assisted racing coach

Section-level feedback for simulator and vehicle performance

A coaching product built for both sim racing and actual racing, combining on-board telemetry with off-board analysis and adapting guidance to driving style.

PM challenge
Create one coaching model that works for simulator sessions, real vehicles, on-board use, and off-board analysis.
Actions
Compared lap sections against the ideal racing line and adapted recommendations to aggressive or conservative driving styles.
Outcome
Section-level feedback that explains where time is gained or lost and turns performance gaps into tailored coaching cues.
